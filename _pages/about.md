I am currently a 5th year PhD student at the Department of Earth and Environmental Sciences of University of Michigan, Ann Arbor (expected graduation: December 2024). I got my bechelor's degree in Geochemistry at the University of Science and Technology of China (2015-2019). My PhD research focuses on lunar science where I develop and employ novel experimental techniques to analyze various targeted Apollo lunar samples originated from both lunar surface and interior and then use computational simulations to model and interpret the experimental data. My PhD thesis specifically aims to improve the understanding of the abundance of volatiles (especitially water) in the lunar mantle by using lunar mineral-hosted melt inclusions and the behaviors of volatile elements during lunar fire-fountain eruptions.
